What is AuraEON

AuraEON is an educational project designed for schools to manage student learning, participation and coexistence. Its goal is to modernize the educational experience through the responsible and efficient use of artificial intelligence.

AuraEON is not a standalone product: it uses Pollo Assistance as its central account and AI system. Students and teachers using AuraEON access AI features through Pollo Assistance.

Relation to Pollo Assistance

The relationship between AuraEON and Pollo Assistance can be understood as follows:

  • Pollo Assistance provides the AI technology, user account system and technical infrastructure.
  • AuraEON provides the educational context, school management tools and classroom-specific features.

Together they form a complete solution for integrating artificial intelligence in education in a safe and controlled way.

Main features

School coexistence management

AuraEON includes tools for schools to manage student coexistence: behavior tracking, incident logging and communication between teachers, students and families.

Learning management

Teachers can use AuraEON to organize content, assign tasks and track their students' academic progress. The integration with Pollo STUDY allows students to access AI-powered study tools directly from the platform.

eduWORLD mode

When Pollo Assistance is used in the context of AuraEON, a special mode called eduWORLD is activated that includes:

  • Differentiated visual theme: Green colors to distinguish the educational environment from personal use.
  • Controlled access: Students access via verification codes provided by the school.
  • Optimized prompts: The AI adjusts its behavior for the classroom context, with more pedagogical responses.
  • Reinforced privacy: Messages in eduWORLD mode are not saved to the database, protecting student privacy.

Responsible AI use

One of the pillars of AuraEON is the responsible use of artificial intelligence in the educational environment. The platform includes:

  • Content moderation system (ai-moderation) to filter inappropriate content.
  • Access control so only authorized students can use the AI.
  • Usage limits adapted to the school context.
  • Transparency in data usage and privacy by design.

AI in service of education

AuraEON and Pollo Assistance work together to bring artificial intelligence to classrooms in a safe, controlled and pedagogically useful way. The goal is not to replace the teacher, but to give them tools to enhance their students' learning.
