Pollo STUDY

Academic module of the Pollo Assistance ecosystem.

TypePollo Assistance module
CategoryEducation and study
AvailabilityFree (basic) / Premium (full)
Target audienceStudents of all levels

What is Pollo STUDY

Pollo STUDY is the Pollo Assistance module designed specifically for the academic environment. It works as a smart study companion that helps students understand, organize and review any subject more efficiently.

Unlike simply copying and pasting questions into an AI chat, Pollo STUDY is optimized with specific instructions for the educational context: it generates pedagogically structured content, adapts explanations to the student's level and offers interactive tools like diagrams and quizzes.

Main features

Summary generation

Pollo STUDY can synthesize long and complex texts from any subject into clear and organized summaries. The student can paste a text, indicate a topic or even upload a document, and the AI generates a structured summary with the key points.

  • Summarizes texts of any length and subject.
  • Organizes information into logical sections.
  • Highlights the most important concepts.
  • Allows requesting different levels of detail.

Exam preparation

One of the most useful features of Pollo STUDY is the generation of quizzes and exam questions. The student specifies the topic and the AI generates multiple-choice questions, essay questions or practical exercises, depending on what they need.

  • Multiple-choice questions.
  • Essay and open-ended questions.
  • Practical exercises adapted to the subject.
  • Correction and explanation of each answer.

Concept explanation

When a student doesn't understand a concept, Pollo STUDY breaks it down into simple terms. The AI adapts its language to the student's level: explaining photosynthesis to a primary school student is not the same as explaining it to a high school student.

  • Clear explanations adapted to the educational level.
  • Use of practical examples and analogies.
  • Ability to delve deeper progressively.
  • Support for any subject or field of study.

Interactive diagrams

Pollo STUDY can generate concept maps and visual diagrams from any topic. These diagrams are interactive: nodes can be expanded and collapsed to explore information at different levels of depth.

  • Automatic generation from text or topic.
  • Expandable and collapsible nodes.
  • Multiple levels of depth.
  • Ability to copy or export the diagram.

Integration with AuraEON

Pollo STUDY is one of the key modules in the integration of Pollo Assistance with AuraEON, the educational project for schools. In the context of AuraEON, Pollo STUDY is used with a special mode (eduWORLD) that includes:

  • Prompts optimized for the classroom context.
  • Differentiated visual theme (green colors).
  • Controlled access through verification codes.
  • Reinforced privacy: eduWORLD messages are not saved to the database.

How to access

Pollo STUDY is available directly from the Pollo Assistance app. When entering the platform, the user can access the STUDY module from the navigation menu or from the quick actions on the welcome screen.

One-time payment, full access

Pollo STUDY is part of the Pollo Assistance ecosystem. With the one-time payment of $25 all advanced module features are unlocked, including the Summum model for deeper analysis. The free plan includes basic access with 20 messages every 6 hours.
